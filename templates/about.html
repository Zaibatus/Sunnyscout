Welcome to Sunnyscout! ☀️

Looking to get away and enjoy some sun? Sunnyscout helps you plan spontaneous trips by comparing destinations by their weather forecast - and up to 9 additional parameters! Whether you're looking for a sunny getaway or a quick city break, we provide data-driven suggestions to make your last-minute trip a breeze!

1) Simply input the dates you can travel, 2) choose your parameters and 3) automatically get the sunniest destinations complete with things to do and foods to try and links to book your trip.

* currently we only are available for European destinations

Have Feedback? Get in Touch!

We love hearing from our users! Whether you have a suggestion or want to report an issue, feel free to drop us a message here.

Our data comes from the below sources:

  • Weather forecast: Open-Meteo API
  • City data: simplemaps
  • Cost data: Numbeo
